Dream of 2017

2016 is great for me, this year i finished my undergraduate degree and made a travel to Myanmar. I went to Myanmar with two of my good friends. Myanmar is a great place for travel, it let me remember my hometown while I was young. I am so familiar to those scenes, such as peaceful and honest people, yelling street peddler in local farmers market, and busy traffic.

My biggest dream is 1. bring my family out for one travel, the potential destination is Hainan. I want them to get out of daily work to have a feeling of different life. My personal dream is 2. to travel to Cuba. I really want to see how a communist country survive in the last 50 years. my last dream is 3. to finish GRE exam.

Life is short, CAN’T wait to fight for it in the 2017.
