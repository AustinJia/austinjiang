Self-Built Autonomous Car

This is one of my favorite project, I spent 80+ hours in this project

Goal:

    Make a autonomous car drive to the brightest spot

Hardware:

  1. Two touch click (left, right) knows there is collision with a obstacle or not
  2. Three Light Sensor (left, center, right)
  3. Two motor
  4. H-Bridge circuits with Arduino to control the motor move forward and backward.
  5. Design in CAD and then laser cutting the wooden board.

Why version 1 failed:

    The design of version 1 was made in CAD, however I failed the conside the tolerance of manufacture. therefore version 1 is too small, the limited space was uable to fit battery and arduino board. Therefore, I made updated version (version 2)
Why version 2 failed:
    failed to aware the difference between servo and motor. The response of light sensor is very fast, however the response of servo is not. In another word, the control of servo is always slower than the radical change of light condition. In addition, for this specific kind of servo that i used in this project, the range of servo’s speed is limited, which means even sent very different speed command to left and right servo, the both servo might runs with equal speed, which made the robot hard to turn.
